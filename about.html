The Good Husband Laundromats & Drycleaners

More than just a laundromat! We are a brand new, unique 24/7 self-service retail lounge concept with a total of 18 of the most state-of-the-art, hi-tech commercial washers and dryers, powered by the IPSO app for even greater value, taking the guesswork out and assuring our patrons when it comes to machines availability and waiting times. Our app also notifies when cycles are about to be completed! In ensuring the highest levels of hygiene, we have dedicated coin-operated washers and dryers for pet garments and soft floor rugs respectively, with frequent sanitisation of all machines carried out numerous times daily.

Aside from the self-service options, we also offer an in-house drop-off and pick-up counter processing laundry and drycleaning daily (except public holidays) with additional services available like ‘Clean & Iron’, ‘Ironing Only’ including the deep cleaning of bags, sneakers and baby strollers!

An exciting and productive experience await our esteemed patrons who will be able to utilise FOC hotdesks to work while they await their laundry, sipping on freshly-ground Robustas, freshly-pressed Australian orange juices and snack on healthy and nutritious ice pops. Our exclusive HelmetSpa machine not only sterilises, sanitises and deodorises helmets, but also soft toys and gloves too, all in 8 minutes!

Don’t take our word for it, come on in and experience it for yourself! Warmly welcoming you to The Good Husband Laundromats & Drycleaners. Always Open. Always Working Hard.
